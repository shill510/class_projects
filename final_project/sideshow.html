Town Bizness

Alcatraz Island is a very well known landmark in the Bay Area for being a federal prison, with a lesser known history of being a native american settlement and later a military fortification. Today, Alcatraz is a popular tourist destination, attracting visitors from around the world who come to explore its rich history and enjoy stunning views of San Francisco Bay.

Pier 39 and Fishermans Wharf are iconic locations in San Francisco that offer a variety of attractions, dining options, and entertainment. Pier 39 is famous for its sea lion population, shopping, and restaurants, while Fishermans Wharf is known for its seafood stalls, historic ships, and lively atmosphere. Both locations provide visitors with a quintessential San Francisco experience.
